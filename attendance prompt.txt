Implement a Google Meet attendance integration system inside the assessment platform.

Architecture:

* Google Meet handles video conferencing.
* The assessment platform handles attendance, analytics, and participation tracking.

Recommended implementation:
Students should NOT join Google Meet directly.
Instead:

1. Teachers create meetings inside the platform.

2. Meetings contain:

   * title
   * subject_id
   * teacher_id
   * class_id
   * Google Meet link
   * meeting date
   * start time
   * end time

3. Students see upcoming meetings on their dashboard.

4. Students click a “Join Meeting” button inside the platform.

5. When the button is clicked:

   * attendance is recorded in the database
   * joined_at timestamp is stored
   * IP address and device info are stored
   * student is redirected automatically to the Google Meet link

Implementation flow:
Student clicks Join Meeting
→ system saves attendance record
→ redirect to Google Meet

* 0 minutes = absent
* 1–10 minutes = partial
* 10–20 minutes = late
* 20+ minutes = present

Implement heartbeat tracking:

* update last_seen timestamp
